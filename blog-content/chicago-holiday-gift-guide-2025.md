## For the Coffee & Tea Lover

**Handmade Ceramic Mugs** ($32-$48)

Chicago ceramic artists create mugs that feel perfectly weighted in hand, have smooth glazes in gorgeous colors, and make every coffee ritual feel special. Look for mugs by makers like Sarah Chen (minimalist white and cobalt blue), Marcus Williams (rustic earth tones), or Elena Rodriguez (playful patterns).

**Why they'll love it**: Each mug is one-of-a-kind, dishwasher safe, and made to last decades—unlike mass-produced mugs that chip after a few months.

Chicago candle makers craft candles that smell exactly like a cozy café—espresso, vanilla, cinnamon, maple. Perfect for anyone who loves coffee but wants to fill their space with the aroma without brewing pot after pot.

## Shopping Tips for Success

**Start Early (or Use Same-Day Pickup)**
While we offer same-day pickup, custom orders and personalized items take 2-6 weeks. Plan ahead for custom gifts, or stick to ready-made items for quick gifting.

**Read Product Descriptions Carefully**
Makers provide detailed info about materials, dimensions, care instructions, and shipping/pickup options. Make sure the item meets your needs.

**Check Pickup Locations**
Same-day pickup locations vary by maker—Logan Square, Pilsen, Wicker Park, etc. Choose makers near you for convenience, or plan a fun neighborhood shopping trip.

**Message Makers with Questions**
Have questions about sizing, colors, customization? Message the maker directly through our platform. Chicago makers are responsive and happy to help.

**Combine Orders When Possible**
If you're buying from multiple makers in the same neighborhood, coordinate pickup times to visit several studios in one trip. It's a fun way to see where your gifts are made.

**Ask About Gift Wrapping**
Many makers offer gift wrapping or beautiful packaging for a small fee. Ask when ordering!
