## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: Why are you giving away your data publicly?

**A:** We believe transparency makes the entire ecosystem stronger. When makers have market intelligence, they price better, stock smarter, and create products buyers actually want. That benefits everyone‚Äîincluding our platform. Data hoarding is a marketplace strategy. Data sharing is infrastructure strategy.

### Q: How is this different from Etsy's seller analytics?

**A:** Etsy shows you YOUR shop data. We show you CHICAGO'S craft economy. That's market intelligence vs individual analytics. Our Index helps makers understand their competitive positioning, seasonal patterns, and neighborhood dynamics‚Äîdata Etsy won't share.

### Q: What's the business model if you're sharing data?

**A:** We charge a commission on sales (competitive with industry). But unlike pure marketplaces, we provide tools that increase seller revenue. Our data shows sellers using our infrastructure tools (Available Today, Price Coach) see 20-35% revenue increases. We win when makers win.

### Q: Is this data accurate?

**A:** Yes. Based on 500+ sellers, 8,400+ listings, and thousands of real transactions. We represent an estimated 15-20% of Chicago's online craft commerce. All data is aggregated (minimum 10 sellers per data point) and anonymized. Privacy-first methodology.

### Q: Will you expand to other cities?

**A:** Yes. Chicago first (prove the model), then Portland, Austin, Minneapolis in 2025-2026. Each city gets its own Index. Hyperlocal data is the moat‚Äîcan't be replicated nationally.

### Q: Can I use this data in my research/article/report?

**A:** Absolutely! Please cite as "Chicago Craft Economy Index, Craft Chicago Finds" and link to craftchicagofinds.com/chicago-craft-index. If you'd like specific data cuts or custom analysis, email press@craftchicagofinds.com.
